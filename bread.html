Cheesy Garlic Bread

Delicious garlic bread you can enjoy with meal!

Cheesy Garlic Bread Ingredients

For this easy garlic bread recipe, you'll need:

  • French or Italian bread
  • Salted butter
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh of granulated garlic
  • Salt and pepper
  • Shredded mozzarella
  • Shredded Parmesan
  • Fresh Parsley

    • How to Make Garlic Bread

    1. Preheat oven and slice bread in half lenghtwise
    2. In a mixing bowl, stir together butter, olive oil, garlic, and salt and pepper
    3. Remove bread from oven and sprinkle with cheese
    4. Bake homemade garlic bread until the cheese is golden and bubbly
